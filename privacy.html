1. Overview

Egypt Unlocked is a bilingual (Arabic/English) travel guide for Egypt. You can browse much of the App as a guest without an account. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices.

2. Information we collect

Account data (only if you sign in):

  • Email address — sign-in, account identification, password reset.
  • Name / basic profile — only if you provide it or it is shared by your sign-in provider (e.g. Google Sign-In).
  • Profile photo (avatar) — only if you upload one; resized (~256px) and stored for your profile.

Content you create: reviews & ratings (stored with your account); favorites and saved trips (stored locally on your device).

Technical/diagnostic: the minimum needed to run the service (e.g. an auth session token in your device's secure storage). No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

We do NOT collect: location (no precise/approximate location tracking); advertising identifiers (no ads, no cross-app tracking); payment data (no in-app purchases).

3. How we use information

To authenticate you, show your profile/avatar, save and display your reviews, sync your account, and answer support. We do not use your data for advertising or profiling.

4. Service providers / third parties

  • Supabase — backend, authentication, and storage (database, auth, avatar storage), protected by row-level security.
  • Google — optional Google Sign-In (we receive your email + basic profile with consent) and Google Places photos for some place imagery/data. Subject to Google's privacy policy.

We do not sell your personal data and share it only to operate the service or as required by law.

5. Data security

Account/content data is stored with Supabase under access controls (RLS); session credentials in secure device storage; favorites/saved trips on your device.

6. Data retention & deletion

We keep account data while your account is active. Request access or deletion via mazn200219@gmail.com. Deleting the App removes local favorites/saved trips.

7. Your rights

Subject to law, you may request access, correction, deletion, or withdraw consent via mazn200219@gmail.com.

8. Children / minors

The App is not directed to children under 13; we do not knowingly collect their data. Contact us to remove any such data.

9. Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date.
